VidyaAI is an educational app designed to help students ask questions and receive answers. We take your privacy seriously and follow these principles: We do not collect any personal information (such as name, phone number, or email) without your explicit consent. All your questions and usage history are stored locally on your device only. We do not share your data with any third-party services. If any feature in the future requires sharing of specific data, your permission will be taken before doing so. Our goal is purely to assist in education, and keeping your data private is our responsibility.
